    The night I didn't take my pills

    Saturday, March 18, 2017

    For that one night, I took a shower, put on pajamas, and got in bed. I listened to a CD to go to sleep, set my alarm, imagined I had a whole world to face tomorrow, but it was standing with me -
    My world.
    And I imagined I was strong without the little pills that let in little thoughts and give me little shakes and make me feel so little.
    I was tall and I was happy, and it wasn’t me and I was wrong to do it.

    Mon Etoilé

    April 4, 2017

    I’ve found a star to call my own
    Alone in the night sky.
    She’s brighter than the rest
    Without having to try.


    She’s a shooting star
    But she hasn’t burnt out yet.
    On this temporary flame
    My heart is fully set.


    I have no reason to call her mine,
    Yet she shines on me alone.
    She’s more than a possession
    And they’re dirty words, “I own.”


    I’ve wished on her too many times
    To separate my soul
    From this star that shines so bright
    And makes me feel whole.


    The star waits for me and I for her,
    Until the end of time and after.
